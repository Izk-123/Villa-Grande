A Legacy of Luxury

Founded in 2010, Villa Grande Lodge has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality in the heart of Malawi. Our commitment to excellence has made us the preferred destination for discerning travelers from around the globe.

We believe that every stay should be a story worth telling. From our meticulously designed rooms to our world-class amenities, every detail is crafted to provide an unforgettable experience.

Our team of dedicated professionals is passionate about creating moments of joy and relaxation for our guests. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, we ensure that your time with us is nothing short of extraordinary.
